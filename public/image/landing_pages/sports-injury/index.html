Sports Inury Treatment

State-Of-The-Art Facilities

Spine Center Atlanta is pleased to offer state-of-the-art, personalized sports injury treatment to the residents of Atlanta and the surrounding areas. We are a comprehensive spine care center located in Buckhead in Atlanta and we specialize in treating all different types of sports-related injuries and conditions.

Our comprehensive treatment plans are designed to help our patients find relief from their pain and other symptoms and return to full function and peak performance levels. Our sports injury treatment plans are also focused on preventing injuries and symptoms and from reoccurring in the future. .

Treatment Options

Spine Center Atlanta offers a wide array of non-surgical and surgical treatments ranging from physical and aquatic therapy to interventional treatments. In many cases, our treatment plans include a combination of therapies and involve other ancillary services such as physical therapy, massage, and acupuncture. For this reason, our facility is fully staffed with physical therapists, massage therapists, and acupuncturists. Our facility also features a physical therapy and rehabilitation center including aquatic therapy as well as a wellness center, on-site.

Our physical therapists, licensed massage therapists, and acupuncturists work under the close guidance of our Orthopaedic sports physicians. This collaborative approach enables us to provide truly comprehensive care, in one convenient and easily accessible location. It also offers our clients more consistency of care and improved overall patient experience.
