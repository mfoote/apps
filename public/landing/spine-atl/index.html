conditions we treat

Pinched Nerve:

We are the experts you can trust to properly diagnose and alleviate your lumbar pinched nerve symptoms. Symptoms of a pinched nerve do not always manifest themselves in the exact location of the nerve. The initial symptoms of a lumbar pinched nerve may include tingling, numbness, a burning sensation, or shooting pains down the buttocks and legs. When there is nerve damage from constant pressure, patients experience increased pain and muscle weakness and may even lose reflexes and movement.

Failed Back Surgery:

Failed Back Surgery Syndrome is not a syndrome at all. Rather, it is a blanket term for those that have undergone back or neck surgery and still find that they have pain. FBSS may also not necessarily mean your surgery truly “failed” in a medical sense. In some cases, your surgery may have been successful, but introduced or uncovered other issues that resulted in additional pain.

If chronic pain persists in any form, even if it changes, it helps to have an expert review your case and see if there are solutions that can be used to treat it.

Extremity Orthopedic Services:

Spine Center Atlanta is pleased to offer state-of-the-art, personalized extremity injury treatment to the residents of Atlanta and the surrounding areas. We are a comprehensive spine care center located in Buckhead in Atlanta and we specialize in treating all different types of sports-related injuries and conditions.

In many cases, our treatment plans include a combination of therapies and involve other ancillary services such as physical therapy, massage, and acupuncture. For this reason, our facility is fully staffed with physical therapists, massage therapists, and acupuncturists. Our facility also features a physical therapy and rehabilitation center including aquatic therapy as well as a wellness center, on-site.
